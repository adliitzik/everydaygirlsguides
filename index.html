40 Easy DIY Christmas Gifts to Make for an Extra-Special Touch

Find edible gifts, homemade jewelry, and more!

By Erin Cavoto
Dec 3, 2020
Ralph Smith

Anyone who's crafty knows how valuable DIY Christmas gifts are. Not only are they fun to make, but your friends and family will feel so touched to have something you made with your own hands. For instance, to find the perfect gifts for your sister, consider something from the heart. Make her a gorgeous knot necklace or homemade bath salts—she'll think of you each time she uses them. On top of being super meaningful, DIY presents are also easy on your wallet—we're talking gifts under $10!

What's better than buying Mom another pine-scented candle? Making one, of course. This roundup includes tons of DIY gifts for Mom that she'll not only use, but she'll cherish. Better yet, try making a gift that's entirely edible—Ree Drummond is a huge fan of this approach! "I love edible gifts! I usually make a few batches of cinnamon rolls or lasagna in disposable tins and deliver them to friends," she says. Check out the ideas for homemade Christmas food gifts, including pancake and hot cocoa mixes. They're perfect for any sugar lovers in your life, and easy on your wallet. And if you're still scratching your head on gifts for dad that he'll truly love and not just say he loves, check out the DIY beer caddy or custom map coasters. They lend such a fun personal touch! Ahead, find more creative Christmas presents you can make at home.
